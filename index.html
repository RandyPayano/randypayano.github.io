Why Data Analytics?

My love for innovation and technology drove me towards the technical field. As an adolescent I was into online gaming and fantasy sports. Monitoring e-gaming and sports statistics day by day, helped me build up an edge and comprehension for numbers. I constantly intrigued in the newest, most advanced video, sound cards, or computer hardware in general. My desire to discover hidden insights, absorb new information, and help pioneer a superior future were all elements in my decision to seek a future in this field. Data Analytics is full of challenges and adventures, and I could certainly say it is my motivation throughout everyday life. As Stephen A. Schwarzman said, "I want to be a telephone switchboard, taking in information from countless feeds, sorting it, and sending it back out into the world."
